About Me

Hello! I’m Sumit Zoting, a passionate Indian wedding photographer based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. With 11+ years of experience and over 300 weddings captured across India, I specialize in documenting vibrant emotions and timeless traditions through meaningful storytelling. From candid moments to grand rituals, my style blends modern creativity with cultural richness, creating unique and heartfelt wedding albums. I focus on building real connections with each couple, ensuring a relaxed, personalized experience from start to finish. Let’s capture your love story beautifully — with honesty, emotion, and artistry.
